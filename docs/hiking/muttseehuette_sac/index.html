Muttseehütte SAC

July 6, 2021 by Pat and Bruno  ‐  5 min read

Difficulty T3
⇄ Distance 11.2 km
↑ Up 730 m
↓ Down 730 m
Notable Locations Tierfed, Kalktrittli, Muttseehütte SAC, Muttenchopf, Limmerensee
Public Transit Access? No

Overview Map Detailed Description Gallery

Quick Summary #

  • Took a (completely over-engineered) cable car from Tierfed to Kalktrittli.
  • From Kalktrittli we took the newly constructed path which had been blown out of the side of the mountain.
    • While the path is quite wide and very well maintained, it is extremely exposed (near-vertical drop of around 800 m).
  • Hiked up towards the Muttenwändli via the Nüschentäli.
    • We had a few small snowfields to cross despite it being mid-summer.
  • Coffee and Wähe at the Muttseehütte SAC.
  • Quick pit-stop on the Muttenchopf.
  • Descent to the Limmerensee.
  • Return via the access tunnel used for constructing the dams at the Limmerensee and the Muttsee.
