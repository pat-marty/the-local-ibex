Windgällenhütte AACZ

On this page

June 9, 2023 by Pat  ‐  2 min read

Difficulty T2
Number of Days 2
⇄ Total Distance 18.9 km
↑ Up 890 m
↓ Down 1250 m
Public Transit Access? Yes

Quick Summary #

  • 2-day hike in the Maderanertal at the Windgällenhütte AACZ.
  • Excellent public transit access.
    • Took the bus from Erstfeld to Golzern, Talstation Seilbahn.
    • Returned via the same bus connection.
  • There were many waterfalls along the length of the valley which, combined with the abundance of lush vegetation, made for some great views.

Day 1: Ascent to the Windgällenhütte AACZ #

Difficulty T2
⇄ Distance 5.3 km
↑ Up 870 m
↓ Down 40 m
Notable Locations Golzern, Bernetsmatt, Windgällenhütte AACZ

Overview Map Gallery

Quick Summary #

  • Took the cable car from Golzern, Talstation to Golzern, Bergstation.
  • Hiked via Bernetsmatt to the Windgällenhütte.
  • Relatively short hiking time to the hut, but this gave us lots of time to explore the area surrounding the hut.

Day 2: Descent via the Chärstelenbach #

Difficulty T2
⇄ Distance 13.6 km
↑ Up 20 m
↓ Down 1210 m
Notable Locations Windgällenhütte AACZ, Firnplanggen, Chärstelenbach, Golzern

Overview Map Gallery

Quick Summary #

  • Took the trail to the east of the Windgällenhütte up the valley.
  • Made a small detour by Holenbalm towards Firnplanggen.
    • While there was indeed a thin trail going towards Firnplanggen, it was not officially marked.
  • Returned to Golzern by taking the trail that ran parallel to the Chärstelenbach.
