Jöriseen

October 28, 2022 by Pat and Bruno  ‐  1 min read

Difficulty T3
⇄ Distance 11.0 km
↑ Up 760 m
↓ Down 760 m
Notable Locations Flüelapass, Winterlücke, Jöriseen, Jöriflüelafurgga
Public Transit Access? Yes

Overview Map Gallery

Quick Summary #

  • An autumn hike near the Flüelapass to the Jöriseen.
  • We parked near the bus stop Wägerhus/Abzw. Jöriseen.
  • Hiked the loop counter-clockwise.
    • First hiked to the Winterlücke and then later up to the Jöriflüelafurgga.
    • We were happy that we chose to do the loop in this orientation since this allowed us to do the crux of the hike (see below) going upwards as opposed to going downwards.
  • We’d rate most of the hike as T2 apart from a single passage on the eastern side of the Jöriflüelafurgga.
    • This spot was the crux of the hike and was secured by a series of steel cables.
    • This passage was quite exposed and was relatively icy when we were there, thus making it somewhat precarious.
  • We made a small detour from the Winterlücke to a small, unnamed peak to the north-west of this small pass.
