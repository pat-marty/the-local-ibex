Gemsfairenstock

July 3, 2022 by Pat and Bruno  ‐  1 min read

Difficulty T4
⇄ Distance 9.0 km
↑ Up 890 m
↓ Down 890 m
Notable Locations Urnderboden, Fisetenpass, Rund Loch, Gemsfairenjoch, Gemsfairenstock
Public Transit Access? No

Overview Map Gallery

Quick Summary #

  • Parked at the cable car in Urnerboden.
    • Took the first cable car up to the Fisetenpass.
  • From the Fisetenpass, we followed the alpine trail (white-blue-white) up to the Rund Loch.
    • The Rund Loch (means “round hole” in German) is a very large window/hole in the side of the mountain.
  • Continued up the alpine trail towards the Gemsfairenjoch.
  • The crux of the hike was a short scrambling passage (~50m) which was secured by chains.
  • From the Gemsfairenjoch, we headed east up the steep ridge to the Gemsfairenstock.
  • Had a brief lunch at the peak with an incredible view of the surrounding mountains (Clariden, Tödi, etc.).
  • Descended the same way that we took on the way up
