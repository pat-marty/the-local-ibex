Munt Baselgia

October 27, 2022 by Pat and Bruno  ‐  2 min read

Difficulty T3
⇄ Distance 15.8 km
↑ Up 740 m
↓ Down 1570 m
Notable Locations Zernez, Munt Baselgia, Spi da Baselgia, Swiss National Park, Macun Seen, Lavin
Public Transit Access? Yes1

Overview Map Gallery

Quick Summary #

  • Hike in the upper Engadin from Zernez to Lavin.
  • We took the Macun Shuttle part way up Munt Baselgia.
    • Driving up this mountain road was not permitted when we were there — taking the alp taxi was the only way up apart from walking.
  • Hiked from the drop-off location up to the Spi da Baselgia (highest point of the hike).
    • Incredible view down into the national park where the Macun Seen (lakes) were.
    • Stopped here for a brief lunch.
  • Continued down over the easter ridge of the Spi da Baselgia and descended into the park to the north.
    • The first portion of the descent was extremely precarious due to recent snowfall.
  • There were very few larches in the national park here given the elevation.
  • Continued down towards Lavin.

  1. There was an alp taxi (the Macun Shuttle) that we took from Zernez up the winding road part way up Munt Baselgia. However, this was not part of the standard SBB timetable and had to be reserved in advance. ↩︎
